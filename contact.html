What to include

Providing these details makes it easier to recommend suitable support and confirm availability.

  • The student’s year group and current level
  • The course, test or university application
  • Relevant examination dates or deadlines
  • Preparation completed so far
  • Current strengths and areas of difficulty
  • Location or time zone
  • Preferred lesson frequency
  • Any urgent timing requirements

A consultation allows us to establish whether the tuition is a good fit before either side commits to a programme of lessons.

Frequently asked questions

Contact and booking questions.

How quickly will I receive a response?

Response time varies with teaching and examination commitments. Please include any urgent deadline clearly in the subject or opening line of your email.

Does sending an enquiry commit me to booking?

No. The initial enquiry and consultation are intended to establish whether the service is suitable before lessons are booked.

Can a parent make the initial enquiry?

Yes. Parents, guardians and students may all make enquiries. Please identify who the tuition is for and include the student’s current level.

Can I enquire through WeChat?

WeChat details can be shared after an initial email enquiry. Please include your WeChat name in the message where relevant.

Are urgent or intensive programmes available?

They may be available depending on timing and existing commitments. Include the examination date and required lesson frequency in your message.
