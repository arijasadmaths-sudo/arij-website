Frequently asked questions

Tuition and booking questions.

Is a regular weekly commitment required?

No. Regular tuition, short preparation programmes and individual specialist sessions may all be available depending on the student's needs.

Why are STEP and interview fees not listed?

These services vary substantially in scope. A programme may involve marking, preparation outside lessons, mock interviews and tailored follow-up work. A clear quotation is therefore provided after discussing the student's requirements.

Can students book a single session?

Yes, where appropriate. Some students book an individual consultation, mock interview or focused problem-solving session, while others benefit from ongoing tuition.

Is work provided between lessons?

For admissions preparation, students are usually given carefully selected work between sessions. The amount depends on their available time and preparation goals.

Are lessons available to students outside the UK?

Yes. Lessons are online and can be arranged for students in different time zones, subject to availability.
